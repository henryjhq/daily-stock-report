## Curriculum

This tracker deepens analysis over time. The skill references this curriculum
to gradually add layers to each report.

**Week 1 — Index Foundations**
- Major indices (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow) and their composition
- Basic sector classification (GICS 11 sectors)
- Key macro indicators: Fed funds rate, 10Y yield, VIX

**Week 2 — Sector Rotation**
- Leading/lagging sectors and capital flows
- Defensive vs cyclical rotation signals
- Treasury yield curve as a signal

**Week 3 — Earnings & Valuation**
- Earnings season dynamics (beats/misses by sector)
- Forward P/E tracking for major indices
- Revenue growth vs margin trends

**Week 4 — Flows & Positioning**
- ETF flows by sector and factor
- Options market signals (put/call ratios, VIX term structure)
- CFTC positioning data (equity futures)
