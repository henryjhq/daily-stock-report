## Curriculum

This tracker is market-agnostic. The skill adapts analysis depth based on
how many entries exist for a given ticker or market.

**Week 1 — Asset Foundations**
- Price history and basic technical levels
- Market cap, sector, and index membership
- Key drivers (rates, currency, commodity, policy)

**Week 2 — Cross-Asset Context**
- Correlation with major indices
- Sensitivity to macro variables (DXY, yields, VIX)
- Relative strength vs peers and sector

**Week 3 — Flow & Sentiment**
- Options market signals if available
- Social sentiment and news volume
- Analyst consensus and recent changes

**Week 4 — Scenario Analysis**
- Bull case: catalysts and price targets
- Bear case: risks and downside triggers
- Event calendar: upcoming dates that matter
